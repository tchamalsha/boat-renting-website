Saltwater Skippers

Walter Matena: A Life on the Water

Walter Matena has always felt at home on the water. Born with an explorer's spirit, he grew up navigating rivers, lakes, and coastal waters, developing an unshakable passion for boating.

His passion led him to acquire Zego Sports Boats, originally founded in New Zealand. Seeing its potential, Walter moved the company to Canada and expanded it into a global brand.

In 2004, Walter moved to Newfoundland and Labrador, captivated by its rugged coastline, hidden coves, and rich maritime history.

A Meeting of Minds: Walter & Didula

In 2025, Walter met Didula Dissanayaka, a businessman from Colombo, Sri Lanka, who had moved to Newfoundland in 2020.

  • Didula had a background in software engineering and was running a successful car rental business in St. John's.
  • Coming from Sri Lanka, an island nation, Didula had always felt a deep connection to the sea.

Both entrepreneurs shared a love for adventure, and together, they saw an opportunity to create something special.

The Birth of Saltwater Skippers

Walter and Didula launched Saltwater Skippers, a water-based adventure company in Conception Bay South (CBS).

The company offers:

  • Boat rentals
  • Guided tours
  • Cod fishing excursions

Expanding the Adventure

The launch in CBS is just the beginning. Walter and Didula plan to expand Saltwater Skippers across Newfoundland.

Future locations will include:

  • Bonavista – dramatic cliffs and breathtaking views
  • Trinity Bay – serene waters and rich maritime history

More Than Just a Boat Rental Service

Saltwater Skippers is about more than just renting boats; it’s about giving people the opportunity to experience the sea in new and exciting ways.

Guests can enjoy:

  • Scenic guided tours
  • Exhilarating fishing excursions
  • Exploring hidden inlets

A Shared Love for the Water

At its core, Saltwater Skippers is a reflection of Walter and Didula’s shared love for the sea.

For Walter, it’s a lifetime spent navigating and sharing his passion for boating.

For Didula, it’s blending his entrepreneurial spirit with his island roots.

With every guest who steps onto a Zego boat, their vision comes to life:

  • To inspire a love for the water
  • To share the unmatched beauty of Newfoundland’s coastline
  • To prove that adventure is just a boat ride away

Co-Owners

Co-owner

Walter Matena

Walter Matena is a passionate entrepreneur with a lifelong love for adventure, innovation, and customer-focused experiences. Raised in Freeport, Bahamas, with German-European roots, his childhood by the ocean fueled his enthusiasm for boating and exploration. His entrepreneurial journey began in 1999 with Beyond Gravity Climbing Gym in Halifax, followed by Rocksolid Recreation, a mobile climbing business in Ontario. He later founded GForce Karting in Newfoundland, expanding it into GForce Funderdome, an indoor adventure park featuring go-karting, mini-golf, axe throwing, and more. Walter's ventures also extend to the water, introducing Whaly Boats to Newfoundland and later acquiring Zego Sports Boats in 2023, relocating its production to Canada in 2024. Combining his love for the ocean with his business expertise, he co-founded Saltwater Skippers, offering Zego Sports Boat rentals and guided tours. Driven by innovation and a customer-first mindset, Walter continues to create unique and thrilling recreational experiences, making adventure more accessible on both land and sea.

Co-owner

Didula Dissanayaka

Born and raised in Sri Lanka, he developed a passion for technology and adventure from a young age. Inspired by robotics, he pursued a degree in Electronics Engineering at the University of Moratuwa. Growing up on an island, he cultivated a deep appreciation for the sea and high-speed activities like karting and water sports. In 2020, he earned a full scholarship to Memorial University of Newfoundland, specializing in AI-driven software for aircraft and UAV navigation. After graduating in 2022, he worked as a software engineer in robotics before transitioning into entrepreneurship. In 2023, he launched a vehicle rental business, followed by an auto repair garage in 2024. The idea for a boat rental business stemmed from his love for adventure and Newfoundland’s stunning coastline. Wanting to offer something unique, he introduced a self-drive experience with high-performance sports boats, allowing customers to explore the waters at their own pace. With a strong foundation in hard work, honesty, and integrity, he combines Sri Lanka’s warmth with Newfoundland’s adventurous spirit. Through this venture, he aims to provide an unforgettable experience, inviting others to embrace the thrill of the open sea.
