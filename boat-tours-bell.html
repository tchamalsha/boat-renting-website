1. Bell Island Thrill Ride!

Prepare for an unforgettable adventure as you take the helm of your very own Zego 300 Sports Boat and embark on a thrilling expedition around the legendary Bell Island! This is not your average boat tour—this is your chance to experience the raw beauty of the North Atlantic like never before. With the ocean breeze in your face and the thrill of the open sea beneath you, you'll navigate through some of Newfoundland’s most breathtaking landscapes, exploring hidden sea caves, towering cliffs, cascading waterfalls, and the rich history of one of Canada’s most fascinating coastal landmarks.

Why This Adventure is a Must-Do!

This is more than just a sightseeing tour—it’s a hands-on experience where YOU are the captain. With expert guides leading the way, you’ll carve through the Atlantic waters, feeling the unmatched power and maneuverability of the Zego 300, a boat designed for both first-time adventurers and experienced thrill-seekers. Stable, virtually unsinkable, and incredibly agile, the Zego 300 makes it easy for anyone to master the waves, whether you’re cruising at a relaxing pace or riding the swells with the open ocean ahead of you.

Not only is this an exhilarating experience, but it’s also an eco-friendly one! The Zego 300 is 100% recyclable and roto-molded for maximum strength, ensuring that your adventure supports the environment while delivering unmatched excitement.

Explore the Untamed Beauty of Bell Island.

Your journey will take you on a 30 km circumnavigation of Bell Island, an Atlantic gem filled with natural wonders and fascinating stories of the past. Along the way, you’ll encounter:

  • Coastal Cliffs & Caves: Towering rock formations shaped by centuries of relentless Atlantic waves.
  • Marine Wildlife: Keep your eyes peeled for whales, porpoises, and seals basking on the rocky outcrops.
  • Seabirds Galore: Puffins, kittiwakes, and bald eagles patrol the skies, searching for their next meal.

History & Folklore

  • Ghostly Legends: Fishermen tell eerie tales of phantom ships seen drifting in the mist around the island.
  • Hidden Pirate Treasure: Could there still be gold buried here from the days of privateers and smugglers?
  • Shipwrecks & Secrets: The surrounding waters have claimed many vessels over the centuries, their wrecks now resting on the ocean floor.

Kelly’s Island: A Coastal Treasure Trove

Spanning approximately 1.5 square kilometers, Kelly's Island is steeped in history and beauty.

What You’ll See:

  • Coastal Cliffs & Caves
  • Marine Wildlife
  • Seabirds Galore

Best Photo Stops:

  • The Southern Cliffs
  • The Hidden Coves

Circumnavigating Kelly’s Island: 5 km

Little Bell Island: A Hidden Gem

Smaller than Kelly’s Island, Little Bell Island is equally captivating with its pristine nature and historical charm.

What You’ll See:

  • Crystal-Clear Waters
  • Seals & Otters
  • Dramatic Rock Faces

Best Photo Stops:

  • The Northern Shoreline
  • The East Cove

Circumnavigating Little Bell Island: 3 km
