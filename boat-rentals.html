Discover the Waters of CBS

Zego 300 Boat Rentals

Saltwater Skippers invites you to explore the stunning waters of Conception Bay South from our Long Pond location. Our Zego 300 boats are perfect for beginners and experienced operators alike. Whether you're seeking a quick adventure or a full-day excursion, our rentals are designed for fun, safety, and ease of use.

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the serene waters of CBS, where you can explore hidden coves, pristine beaches, and the charming islands that dot the bay. Notable spots include Kelly's Island and Bell Island, each offering unique landscapes and rich histories. Whether you're interested in fishing, photography, or simply soaking in the breathtaking scenery, our Zego 300 boats provide the perfect platform for your adventure.

Why Choose the Zego 300?

1. Unmatched Safety & Stability

The Zego 300 is designed with your safety and comfort in mind. Its unique catamaran-style hull provides exceptional stability on the water, giving you a smooth and secure ride. This innovative design minimizes rocking and ensures the boat stays level, even in choppy conditions, making it perfect for both first-time operators and experienced adventurers.

Built using advanced roto-molded construction, the Zego 300 is incredibly durable and impact-resistant. This seamless, one-piece design enhances buoyancy and eliminates weak points, ensuring a safer and more reliable experience on the water.

Roto-molded boats like the Zego 300 are not only tough but also environmentally friendly—100% recyclable and crafted with sustainability in mind. At Saltwater Skippers, we are proud to choose Zego boats because they align with our commitment to protecting the beautiful marine environment of Newfoundland & Labrador for future generations.

Whether you're cruising the shoreline or navigating gentle waves, the Zego 300's thoughtful engineering makes it easy to handle, smooth to ride, and safe for all levels of experience. Zego 300's catamaran-style hull provides superior stability, reducing the risk of tipping, even in choppy waters. This makes it ideal for first-time operators and seasoned riders alike.

2. Beginner-Friendly Operations

Simple controls and easy maneuverability make the Zego 300 a breeze to operate. Our pre-rental safety briefing ensures you are comfortable and confident on the water.

3. Adventure-Ready for Experienced Riders

With responsive handling and robust performance, the Zego 300 is built for those who want to push their limits and explore further.
